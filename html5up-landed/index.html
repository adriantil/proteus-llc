Veteran Powered

Training and employing veteran cyber professionals

We believe one solution to bridging the cybersecurity talent gap is mentoring and training transitioning veterans. Many veterans leave the service with advanced degrees and certifications. We help them land on their feet by leveraging up to six month internships and OJT, where they can apply their technical skills to real world problems. Once the program is done, we hire them or help them find employment through our network at no cost to other organisations.
